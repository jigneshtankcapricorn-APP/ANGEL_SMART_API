Project: ANGEL_SMART_API — A Streamlit app connected to Angel One API for stock market analysis.

Problem You Had:
You use the app on mobile. When you switch to another app or lock the screen, the background data fetch stops and all data is lost. This was the main problem we needed to solve.

What We Built / Fixed:
1. Watchlist Page (2_Watchlist.py) — Completely Redesigned
The page is now split into two clear sections:
First section is Update CSV. You press the "📥 Update CSV" button once before or after market hours. It fetches LTP, 52W High, 52W Low, and Volume for all 2242 stocks from Angel One and saves everything to a file called stocks_live.csv inside your data folder. The CSV saves automatically every 50 stocks during the scan so even if the session dies midway, your data is not lost. Last update date and time is always shown next to the button.
Second section is Watchlist. You set your filters in the sidebar like 5% to 20% above 52W low, market cap 1000cr, sector etc. Then press the "🔍 Fetch from CSV" button. It reads instantly from stocks_live.csv with zero API call. You can change filters and press Fetch again as many times as you want. No Angel One connection needed to view data.
2. Signals Page (3_Signals.py) — Save to Monitor Added
At the bottom of the Signals page there is now a multiselect dropdown showing all visible stocks. You select the stocks you want to observe and click "📌 Save to Monitor". Selected stocks are saved permanently to a file called monitor_stocks.csv. This solves the problem of losing stocks when you change your Watchlist filter from 5-10% to 20-25%.
3. New Monitor Page (5_Monitor.py) — Brand New Page
This page shows all stocks you saved from the Signals page. It displays Symbol, Sector, Signal, Entry Price, Current Price, Percent Change, Days Held, Swing High, Swing High Date, EMA23, Stop Loss, Risk and Volume Ratio. There is a "🔄 Refresh Current Prices" button to fetch live prices when you want. There is a Remove button to delete any stock you no longer want to monitor. Data is saved permanently in monitor_stocks.csv and stays there no matter how many times you change Watchlist or Signals filters.

Daily Routine Going Forward:
Before or after market, open app on PC, press "📥 Update CSV" and wait about 45 minutes. You do not need to watch it. After that, open on mobile anytime, set filters, press "🔍 Fetch from CSV" and results appear instantly. If you find good stocks on the Signals page, select them and press "📌 Save to Monitor" before changing any filters.
